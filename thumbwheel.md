# Thumb Wheel Specification

## Dimensions
- OD: 25mm
- Height: 10mm

## Thread
- M6 coarse (pitch 1.0mm), internal, printed
- Full depth through wheel
- Implementation: bd_warehouse `IsoThread`

## Countersink (top face)
- For M6 flat-head bolt
- OD at surface: 12mm
- Depth: 4mm
- Profile: conical

## Grip
- 9 vertical flutes evenly spaced around circumference
- Profile: semi-circular (cylinder cutter, r=3.5mm, centre on outer surface)
